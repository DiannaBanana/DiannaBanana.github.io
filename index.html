Day-Trading Distrbuted System

My team and I were contracted by DayTrading Inc. (a secret organization) to develop a prototype end-to-end solution to support their day trading clients and services.


Currently, my team and I are designing and implementing an online day-trading website to demonstrate software scalability techniques for up to 1500 concurrent users.
